FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I book a room?

Most likely, the room is not available and is occupied by somebody else. If a room is available and you cannot book it please contact us.

Can I book a room without scanning the QR code or in advance?

No, this app helps students book rooms when they are looking for them in the location they want to book a room in.
